HR Attrition Workforce Intelligence

Project Description / Purpose  
This project analyzes workforce attrition trends to understand employee movement, workforce stability, and areas of organizational risk using interactive business intelligence reporting.

Tech Stack  
- Excel  
- Power BI  
- DAX  
- GitHub  

Data Source  
The HR workforce dataset includes employee demographics, experience levels, role details, compensation data, and attrition status.

Features / Highlights

Business Problem  
Unplanned workforce attrition negatively impacts productivity, workforce planning, and operational continuity. Leadership often lacks visibility into the drivers of attrition at the workforce level.

Goals  
- Analyze trends in workforce attrition  
- Identify high-risk employee segments  
- Support workforce planning and retention strategies  

Key Visuals / Description  
- Overview of the workforce attrition rate  
- Attrition rates by department and job role  
- Attrition based on experience and tenure  
- The impact of compensation on attrition  
- Key performance indicators for workforce stability  

Business Impact  
- Improved accuracy in workforce planning  
- Reduced risk of employee turnover  
- Data-driven strategies for employee retention  

Insights  
- The early-career workforce experiences higher attrition rates.  
- Certain departments consistently contribute to employee exits.  
- Experience and compensation levels significantly influence workforce stability.
